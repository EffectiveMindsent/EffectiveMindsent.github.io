Basic concepts of the courses

Electrician

An electrician is a professional specialized in carrying out installations and repairs related to electricity, with particular focus on machines and lighting. It is important not to confuse it with a lineman, which in some countries may also be called an electrical technician.

Carpenter

Prepare, assemble and repair doors, windows and make wall and ceiling coverings, stairs and custom carpentry furniture, in the workshop or "on site" based on the technical information or instructions received, develop the construction project and budget

Plumber

A plumber or plumber is a professional specialized in installing and repairing pipes that supply water, gas and air, with knowledge of installations of drainage and heating systems in residential, commercial and home complexes, or in any other urban development work.

Painter

A painter (feminine, painter) is an artist who practices the art of painting, which consists of creating paintings and frescoes, in a manner considered artistic which can communicate some aesthetic enjoyment, emotions, feelings, or references of social, historical and cultural context.

Welder

MISSION Welders and flame cutters weld and cut pieces of metal by welding and cutting it, or welding and melting it, and forming a filler metal between the joints, using, for this purpose, an electric arc.

Heavy machine operator

A heavy equipment operator is a professional who operates and maintains large, powerful equipment, such as excavators, cranes, graders, pavers, service trucks, and planers.

Driver

All vehicles that circulate on public roads need a driver. The driver is the person who handles the steering mechanism or is at the controls.

Automobile mechanic

The functions performed by an automotive mechanic are mainly related to the technical inspection of a vehicle. Typically, these functions encompass vehicle diagnostics, preventative maintenance, disassembly, component replacement, repair, and assembly.

Hotel receptionist

A hotel receptionist is the professional who is in charge of receiving and serving hotel guests. It is the main point of contact between the guest and the hotel.

Waiter

Waiters prepare tables in a restaurant before customers arrive, take orders, serve food and drink, and clean tables.

Bartender

A bartender is a professional who prepares and serves drinks in bars, restaurants, hotels, canteens, cafes and clubs.

Chef

A Chef is a professional who is in charge of preparing, preserving, and presenting food. You can work independently or in a company.

Stylis

A stylist is a professional who is responsible for creating or improving the image of a person or a product. You can work in the world of fashion, beauty, cinema, magazines, companies, among others.

Cosmetology

Cosmetology is a branch of aesthetics that deals with the beauty and care of skin, hair and nails. Cosmetologists advise their clients on how to care for their skin, hair and nails.

Cashier

A cashier is a person who is responsible for receiving and processing payments in commercial establishments. It is also known as a store cashier, supermarket cashier, restaurant cashier or administrative cashier.

Customer service advisor

A customer service advisor is a professional who is responsible for solving customer problems, answering questions, and providing information about products or services.

Warehouseman

A warehouseman is a person who is responsible for the management of a warehouse, either as an owner or as an employee.

Security guard

A security guard is a professional who is responsible for the protection and security of people and property. He is also known as a security guard.

Tattoo artist

A tattoo artist is an artist who uses needles and pigments to etch permanent images into people's skin.

Gardener

A gardener is a person who is dedicated to caring for and maintaining gardens, parks, green areas, among others.

Youtuber

A youtuber or youtuber is a producer and creator of audiovisual content who uses YouTube as his or her communication platform. ​ Some YouTubers have corporate sponsors who pay for product placement in their videos or production of online ads.

Digital marketing

Digital marketing is the use of digital channels to promote products and services. It uses strategies such as SEO, social media advertising, email marketing and content marketing.

Content marketing

Content marketing is a marketing strategy that consists of creating and distributing content to attract and retain a target audience.

B2B Marketing

B2B (business-to-business) marketing is the strategy that companies use to market their products or services to other companies. The goal is to contact the other company's decision makers, rather than the end consumer.

B2C Marketing

B2C (Business to Consumer) marketing is the set of strategies that companies use to sell their products or services directly to consumers.

Outbound marketing

Outbound marketing is a promotional strategy that seeks to contact potential customers to get them interested in a product or service. It is also known as outbound marketing or "traditional marketing."

Children's influencers

Children's influencers are boys and girls who create content for social networks such as TikTok and Instagram, reaching a large audience.

Teenage influencers

Teenage influencers are people who influence the purchasing decisions of young people through social media.

Adult influencers

Adult influencers, also known as “senior influencers” or “grandfluencers,” are people over the age of 50 who create content on social media. Through their publications, they share their experiences, passions and perspectives with a global audience..

Artificial intelligence (AI)

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a field of computing that allows computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
